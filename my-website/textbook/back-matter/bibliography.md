# Bibliography

## Books

1. Siciliano, B., & Khatib, O. (2016). *Springer Handbook of Robotics*. Springer. This comprehensive handbook covers all aspects of robotics including kinematics, dynamics, planning, and control.

2. Spong, M. W., Hutchinson, S., & Vidyasagar, M. (2006). *Robot Modeling and Control*. Wiley. A foundational text on robot modeling, trajectory planning, and control systems.

3. Thrun, S., Burgard, W., & Fox, D. (2005). *Probabilistic Robotics*. MIT Press. Covers probabilistic approaches to robotics including localization, mapping, and planning.

4. Goodfellow, I., Bengio, Y., & Courville, A. (2016). *Deep Learning*. MIT Press. The definitive text on deep learning, including applications to physical systems.

5. Craig, J. J. (2005). *Introduction to Robotics: Mechanics and Control*. Pearson. A classic text on robot mechanics, kinematics, and control.

## Journal Articles

6. Khatib, O. (1986). Real-time obstacle avoidance for manipulators and mobile robots. *The International Journal of Robotics Research*, 5(1), 90-98. The seminal paper on artificial potential fields for robot navigation.

7. LaValle, S. M. (1998). Rapidly-exploring random trees: A new tool for path planning. *TR 98-11*, Computer Science Department, Iowa State University. Introduction of the RRT algorithm for motion planning.

8. Fox, D., Burgard, W., & Thrun, S. (1997). The dynamic window approach to collision avoidance. *IEEE Robotics & Automation Magazine*, 4(1), 23-33. A key paper on real-time collision avoidance for mobile robots.

9. Kaelbling, L. P., Littman, M. L., & Moore, A. W. (1996). Reinforcement learning: A survey. *Journal of Artificial Intelligence Research*, 4, 237-285. A comprehensive survey of reinforcement learning techniques.

10. Thrun, S. (2002). Robotic mapping: A survey. *Exploring Artificial Intelligence in the New Millennium*, 1, 1-35. A comprehensive survey of robotic mapping techniques.

## Conference Papers

11. Kavraki, L. E., Švestka, P., Latombe, J. C., & Overmars, M. H. (1996). Probabilistic roadmaps for path planning in high-dimensional configuration spaces. *IEEE Transactions on Robotics and Automation*, 12(4), 566-580. The original paper on Probabilistic Roadmaps (PRM).

12. LaValle, S. M., & Kuffner Jr, J. J. (2001). Randomized kinodynamic planning. *The International Journal of Robotics Research*, 20(5), 378-400. Introduction of RRT for kinodynamic systems.

13. Fox, D., Burgard, W., Kruppa, H., & Thrun, S. (2001). A probabilistic approach to collaborative multi-robot localization. *Autonomous Robots*, 11(3), 225-244. Key paper on multi-robot localization.

14. Quinlan, S., & Khatib, O. (1993). Elastic bands: Connecting path planning and control. *Proceedings of the IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation*, 802-807. A method for connecting path planning to control.

15. Anguelov, D., et al. (2005). Learning to detect objects in images via a sparse, part-based representation. *Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems*, 17, 127-134. A key paper on object detection in images.

## Technical Reports and Standards

16. ISO 13482:2014 - Robots and robotic devices -- Personal care robots. International Organization for Standardization. Safety requirements for personal care robots.

17. ISO 10218-1:2011 - Robots and robotic devices -- Safety requirements for industrial robots -- Part 1: Robots. International Organization for Standardization. Safety requirements for industrial robots.

18. IEC 62061:2005 - Safety of machinery - Functional safety of safety-related electrical, electronic and programmable electronic control systems. International Electrotechnical Commission.

19. ISO 26262-6:2018 - Road vehicles -- Functional safety -- Part 6: Product development at the software level. International Organization for Standardization.

20. IEC 61508-2:2010 - Functional safety of electrical/electronic/programmable electronic safety-related systems -- Part 2: Requirements for electrical/electronic/programmable electronic safety-related systems. International Electrotechnical Commission.

## Online Resources

22. Robot Operating System (ROS) Tutorials. http://wiki.ros.org/. Tutorials and documentation for the Robot Operating System.

23. TensorFlow Documentation. https://www.tensorflow.org/. Official documentation for the TensorFlow machine learning framework.

24. Python Robotics Library. https://github.com/AtsushiSakai/PythonRobotics. Open-source Python code for robotics algorithms.
