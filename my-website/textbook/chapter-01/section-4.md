# 1.4 Prerequisites

This textbook is designed for **beginners and intermediate learners**.
You only need:

- Basic programming knowledge (Python recommended)
- Curiosity to explore how robots think and move
- Optional: Basic understanding of electronics

You **do not** need advanced math or prior robotics experience.
We start from fundamentals and build your knowledge step by step.
