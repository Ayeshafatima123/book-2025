# 1.5 Getting Started

To get the best learning experience:

1. Follow chapters in sequence.
2. Complete hands-on exercises at the end of each chapter.
3. Experiment with provided code snippets.
4. Build or simulate your own physical AI robot.
5. Document your results and improvements.

Practical learning is the heart of this textbook.
