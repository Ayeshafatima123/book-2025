# 1.1 What is Physical AI?

**Physical AI** refers to artificial intelligence designed to operate in and interact with the physical world.

It combines concepts from:

- **Robotics Engineering**
- **Mechanical & Electrical Systems**
- **Sensors and Real-world Data**
- **Computer Vision & Perception**
- **Machine Learning**
- **Control Theory**
- **Motion & Path Planning**

Unlike traditional AI, which exists only in software, **Physical AI connects intelligence to movement** — allowing machines to:

- see the world,
- understand it,
- make decisions,
- and act physically.

## Real-world examples of Physical AI

- **Humanoid robots** that walk, talk, and adapt
- **Drones** that navigate cities
- **Self-driving cars** that detect obstacles
- **Warehouse robots** sorting and transporting goods
- **AI-powered medical robots** assisting doctors

Physical AI is the foundation behind the future of robotics and autonomous systems.
